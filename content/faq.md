H1	Frequently Asked Questions — ARSK & Associates

Questions we are often asked

Who does ARSK typically work with?
We work with founders and promoters of growing Indian companies, family businesses managing succession or governance transitions, CFOs of mid-market enterprises, multinationals entering or operating in India, and non-resident Indians with financial and compliance obligations in India.

What is the typical engagement model?
Most clients work with us on an ongoing retainer basis — for audit, tax compliance, or corporate law advisory. We also take on specific project engagements: a transaction requiring due diligence, a valuation assignment, a tax dispute representation, or a one-time restructuring advisory.

Do you handle GST and indirect tax matters?
Yes. Our indirect tax practice — headed by Ravindra Khandelwal — covers GST advisory, return filing, departmental audit response, show-cause notice management, and appellate representation.

Can you help with a tax dispute that is already in progress?
Yes. We frequently step in on matters that are already at the assessment, appeal, or tribunal stage. We will need to understand the history of the matter before we commit to a view, but we are experienced in taking over and managing live disputes.

We are a foreign company entering India. Can you help with entity setup, tax registration, and ongoing compliance?
Yes. We provide end-to-end India entry advisory, covering entity structure advice, tax and FEMA considerations, company incorporation support, tax registrations, and ongoing compliance management.

Do you provide outsourced CFO or finance function services?
Yes. Our virtual CFO division provides outsourced finance leadership for growing companies — covering financial planning, MIS, board reporting, regulatory compliance, and coordination with auditors and lenders.

We are an NRI. What services do you offer?
We advise NRIs on Indian income tax obligations, DTAA planning, remittance certifications, FEMA compliance, and investment structuring. If you have inherited property in India, are planning to return, or have regular income from Indian sources, we can help clarify your obligations and optimise your position.
